About Us

OUR LEGACY: MORE THEN JUST BEER

Founded more than 165 years ago, Anheuser-Busch is one of America’s most iconic companies and the undisputed leader of the U.S. beer industry. We are proud of our history and heritage and remain committed to brewing the great-tasting, high-quality beers that have satisfied beer drinkers for generations. Today, we are 18,000 employees colleagues strong—spread out across the country, united by a deep passion for making great beer and an unwavering commitment to supporting the communities that we call home. We want to share with you our pride in what we do, who we are, and what our vision is for the future of our company – this is what gives us purpose beyond brewing.

KLEENEX WET WIPES AND BUDWEIZER

Through unforeseen events beginning early 2020, we have collaborated with Kleenex wet wipes to promote an exceptionally clean beer to keep you company through this troubling time. There is projected to be 3 million limited edition bottles distributed within the next month. We are now offering delivery services. We are also using six of our craft breweries to devote strictly to making sanitizer for products such as hand sanitizer and wet wipes. By using a different recipe and different process but using the same principles that we are already very comfortable with. Fighting this is better working hand in hand, together, as a team, as one nation.

“It is my aim to win the American people over … to make them all lovers of beer.” - Adolphus Busch (1905)

ONE NATION, TOGETHER WE STAND

Until further notice, for every beer bottle sold a meal will be provided to non profits in our communities as well as our distributor partners and employees. In addition we will be giving back to bartenders and service industry workers effected by the restaurant and bar closure caused by these unforeseen events. Approximately one million dollars will be donated to the United States Bartenders’ Guild, helping bartenders and other industry professionals. Fighting this is a nation-wide call, one that we will win walking hand in hand. Together we stand, the United States of America.

QUALITY TO COMMITMENT

For more than 165 years, our teams across the country have been united by a passion for brewing the great-tasting, high-quality beers that have satisfied beer drinkers for generations. Our rigorous quality assurance program covers every step of our supply chain, from seed to sip. We work closely with our grower partners to ensure we source only the finest ingredients for our beers. Our brewmasters then carefully oversee every detail in the brewing process to ensure consistency, and taste every batch before it is packaged. Finally, we conduct over 100 different package quality checks before any product leaves any of our facilities.
