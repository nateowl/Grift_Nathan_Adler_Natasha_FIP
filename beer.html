Our Secret

Hops: the ’Spice’ of beer

To create the distinctive taste, only the supplest, most aerobic hops are selected.

Yeast: The 'life' of beer

Yeast turns sugar into alcohol, triggering unique smells and tastes. It adds powerful aroma to beer.

Rice: for a crisp clean Finish

We brew our lager using fresh, verdant rice-milled, polished, graded and immediately brewed for a crisp taste and smooth finish

Barley: the ‘soul’ of beer

Our beer is brewed with only the finest two-row and six-row barley malt, hand-selected from regional growers across America.
